About Us

We want everyone on the road to be well protected, at any time. SwitchIn is a free, unbiased auto insurance evaluation tool that provides you with the best customized solution. We do this by making sure you understand your current coverage and suggesting a better alternative if needed.

With SwitchIn, you will:
- Learn everything you need to know about your coverage in a few minutes
- Get your coverage ranked and analyzed in detail based on your vehicle type and location
- Receive suggestions (IF you need them) on what you could improve and WHY!

Available in the state of California only at this moment.
To start, go to the home page and upload a copy of your “proof of insurance”, or manually enter your coverage information.

- That’s it. Watch the magic happen.
